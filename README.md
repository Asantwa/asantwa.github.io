# Asantwa Smith | SWE Coach

Personal landing page for Asantwa Smith's software engineering interview coaching.

Live site: https://asantwa.github.io

## What This Site Is For

This site is for software engineers, new grads, career switchers, and early-to-mid career technologists who want clearer, more confident interview prep.

Visitors can use the site to:

- Learn what kind of interview support Asantwa offers
- Understand the coaching style and areas of focus
- Book a 1:1 call
- Send an intro with their current level, target roles, and biggest prep challenges

## What Leads Can Expect

The coaching experience is designed to be practical, direct, and human. The goal is to help candidates understand where they are getting stuck and turn that into a focused prep plan.

Common focus areas include:

- Resume and LinkedIn positioning
- Technical interview communication
- DSA practice strategy
- Behavioral interview storytelling
- Mock interview feedback
- Prep plans for upcoming interview loops
